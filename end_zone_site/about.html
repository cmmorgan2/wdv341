OUR STORY

Dennis and Neil have been friends since high school. They played on the same team together until Dennis was injured in his senior year. After graduation, they decided to start a small business with a humble website to sell sports apparel. From there, EZ APPAREL grew into what it is today.

OUR TEAM

Dennis LaMont co-founded the End Zone brand in 2010, but took the lead on the vision. As a fellow sports fanatic, he understands the importance of good, comfortable sports gear and that is where END ZONE APPAREL comes in. "Never Compromise on Quality."

Neil Andersen, Co-Founder, is in charge of our business department and makes sure that END ZONE APPAREL is running to the best of its ability. He has been with us for twelve years and has seen our small company thrive. "Always do your best."

Camilla Statford is our Marketing Lead and also in charge of company branding. She makes sure that everything is on brand and beautiful. Our brand benefits daily from her eye for detail and unparalleled sense of design and form. "A warm smile starts the day."
